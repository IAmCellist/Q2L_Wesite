Our goal is that Quest2Learn will provide students with an interactive way to learn laboratory techniques through a simulated lab learning experience in augmented reality and a "gamification" approach.

Our simulations are developed hand in hand with faculty at JHU based on their lab courses to ensure content accuracy and credibility.



What We Do

Quest2Learn is an augmented reality (AR) application that enhances both online and on-campus lab sciences education. Through Quest2Learn, we aim to address the lack of access to hands-on lab learning during the pandemic.
